Wedding Details

First and foremost, we just want to say THANK YOU for being part of our special day! You were invited for a good reason- because we are very happy that you're a part of our life! We are glad you can attend and we hope you'll have lots of fun. Here are some more details about our wedding, so you know what to expect.

Guests & Attire

There will be around 50 guests total- we'd like to keep the wedding small. It will mostly be the bride's friends & family, but you will get to meet the groom's parents and best friend. (We will be having another celebration in the Netherlands for his side of the family.) You may bring someone with you if you'd like, but you must let us know so we can be sure we have enough food & chairs. We reserve the right to accept or deny your request to bring someone (although we probably won't say no, since cool people mostly know other cool people.)

We aren't strict about attire, just come in something mildly fancy, as if you're dressing up for a nice church service. Be comfortable, and dress for the weather on the big day! Our only rule is no long white dresses allowed- that's reserved for the bride.

Reception

The reception will be at the same location as the wedding, so no need to get up and drive somewhere else. It will be in a nearby field, with tables, chairs, food, music, the whole shabang. The reception will be from 5-8 pm.

Food & Beverages

There will be a catering service and drinks available- both alcoholic and non. If you plan on drinking alcohol, please find a designated driver beforehand, or use a LYFT/Uber. If you have any dietary restrictions or allergies, we can't gurantee that the food provided by the catering service will accommodate, so bring something for yourself just in case. The catering will mostly be delicious snacks and sweets, NOT a full meal, so plan your day accordingly as the wedding begins at 4:00 pm and the reception will be around 5-8 pm.

Wedding Gifts

We sincerely appreciate any gifts you give us. They're absolutely NOT required, though. Your presence means the most to us! That said, should you wish to give us something, a contribution towards our honeymoon fund would mean the world to us, as there's only so much space in our suitcase. We'd also love any small keepsakes, sentimental things, or a sweet card. You could even just bring a snack or drinks! Contrubuting something small to the big day would be wonderful, but again obviously not required. Breakable things aren't guranteed to survive the trip home, nor are any electronics which won't work in Europe. Thank you so much for understanding!

Parking & Transportation

There will be plenty of parking available. It's a short, flat and accessible walk to the actual park. If you don't have a ride, let us know and we can help you figure something out.

Questions?

We hope this was helpful, but if you have further questions that are not specified on here, please let us know. Thank you for taking time out of your weekend for us! It means the world that you can be here. We look forward to celebrating with you!
