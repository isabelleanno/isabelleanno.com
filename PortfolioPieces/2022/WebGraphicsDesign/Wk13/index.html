Week 13 Typography

My Process:

I used InkScape to make this logo. I started with a reference photo of a baby elephant that I found on Google Images, and then from there I traced it, modified it and simplified it until it became this cute little minimalist outline. I'm trying to convey a soft, safe feeling with the typography and color scheme. I was thinking this would be a cute baby clothing brand, maybe specializing in boy's clothes, or it could be unisex. The font I used is a bit playful and has a slight hand-written feel to it, but it also maintains a clean professional look, and wrapping the text around the logo ties it all together neatly.
