The Book Nook

Library, book store & so much more!

We provide affordable access to literature and education in inner city areas

Weekly Classes

Learn a variety of practical skills and meet new people along the way! For only $7 per class, you will master all the things school never taught you, like how to maintain a proper diet, create (and stick to) a budget, or the importance of mental health. Each class is unique, so sign up now!

Book Store

Support our local businesses at the book store, where you can buy the latest novels across many genres. Our books are brand new or gently used and discounted. We primarily sell fiction and comic bookk but we also have historical and scientific books for personal knowledge and studying.

Library

Our library membership allows you to:

  • Check out our books
  • Use the computer room during open hours
  • Get a discount on weekly classes- $4 instead of $7.

Our cards are $15 for a three-month pass, or $50 for a year pass. We take gently used book donations as well. Thank you for supporting us and keeping the weekly classes at a low price for the public!
