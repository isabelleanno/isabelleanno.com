🐾I love my dog Henry!

About Henry:

Henry is 15 years old! We rescued him from a shelter when he was a puppy. He's like my younger brother! I grew up with him. I'm fond of all my pets, but he's got a special place in my heart.


Henry is a cockapoo, which is a mix between a cocker spaniel and a poodle. He is very intelligent, charming, and funny. He's super well-behaved and would never hurt a fly. Henry loves small animals like mice, insects and ferrets. He's very gentle around them, and adores their cuteness with us.

Learn more about cockapoos here!

4 years ago, we adopted another cockapoo puppy named Reggie. Henry helped us raise him to be the best of boys, and he is! They still play together, and Reggie helps keep Henry young.

I'm so blessed to have such a great dog!
